# Summary
This paper introduces a novel condition monitoring methodology for estimating parameters like thickness, web roll density, and inertia within roll-to-roll systems. By integrating physics-based models with real-time data facilitated by digital twin technology, the authors aim to enable precise quality tracking and process optimization without relying heavily on physical sensors. The approach is validated through experiments demonstrating its effectiveness in monitoring roll qualities and improving control parameters.
