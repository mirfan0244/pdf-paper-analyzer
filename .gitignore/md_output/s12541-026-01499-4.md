Illy-Net is a novel dual-flow evaluation and calibration framework that efficiently selects optimal candidates from multiple simulation-trained policies using only a small amount of real-world data. It simultaneously achieves the efficiency of simulation-based learning and the reliability of real-world validation, making it a practical solution for safety-critical or cost-sensitive deployments in industrial environments.
