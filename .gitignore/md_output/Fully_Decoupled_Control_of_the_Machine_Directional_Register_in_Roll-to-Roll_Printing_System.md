# Abstract Summary

This research proposes an innovative approach in Roll-to-Roll (R2R) printing systems by introducing a fully decoupled proportional-derivative (FDPD) controlled algorithm. Focused on eliminating the adverse effects of upstream control variables on downstream printing accuracy, this study presents a mathematical model that quantifies these interactions. The FDPD algorithm achieves complete coupling removal, significantly enhancing pattern alignment precision across multiple printing units without large fluctuations, proving its effectiveness and ease of implementation within industrial settings.
