The paper provides a comprehensive review of theories and control technologies in roll-to-roll manufacturing processes. It discusses the challenges posed by the viscoelastic nature and sensitivity to thermal deformations and offers insights into tension and lateral error controls, emphasizing the importance of precision for successful production outcomes. The study summarizes advancements in modeling techniques and controllers, underscoring their critical roles in enhancing product quality in printed electronics manufacturing.
