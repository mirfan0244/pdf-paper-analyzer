This study presents a control strategy for web tension in roll rewinder systems using an Auto-Tuning PI controller augmented by Fuzzy Logic. Traditional issues such as wrinkles and uneven surfaces are mitigated by stabilizing the web tension, leading to enhanced paper quality. The research combines mathematical modelling, simulation, and experimental testing methodologies. It demonstrates that this approach effectively reduces overshoots and steady-state errors, thereby improving control system performance in managing the dynamics of paper production processes.
