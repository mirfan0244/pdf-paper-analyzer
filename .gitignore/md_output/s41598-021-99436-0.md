### Summary
This research article presents the fabrication of IoT devices using a roll-to-roll (R2R) screen printing process. This method involves multi-layer circuit platform production with printed conductive and insulation layers, facilitating efficient IoT device assembly for mass production. The study aims to enhance manufacturing efficiency by reducing time and cost while leveraging advanced screen printing techniques alongside online processes like sintering and curing. Key aspects involve managing the alignment of overlay printed layers and optimizing electrical conductivity through adjustments in materials and print methods. Successful implementation showcases a novel, scalable approach for developing IoT and flexible electronics applications.
