### Markdown Summary

The paper presents a robust design of PI control for web winding systems in reversible cold rolling mills using adaptive backstepping. The control law aims to achieve high precision in managing web velocity and tension, crucial for avoiding the deformation or breakage of metal products during processing. The proposed method handles unknown parameters and external disturbances effectively by incorporating integral action into the adaptive backstepping process. Stability is ensured via Lyapunov's theorem, with MATLAB/Simulink simulations demonstrating the controllers' effectiveness in enhancing product quality and production efficiency.
