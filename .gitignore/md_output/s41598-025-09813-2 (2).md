# Summary

This paper introduces an innovative AI-driven digital twin approach for optimizing web tension in Roll-to-Roll (R2R) manufacturing systems. By leveraging Bayesian optimization techniques combined with Gaussian process modeling, the study proposes a framework that autonomously adjusts control parameters to achieve optimal system performance. The methodology allows for real-time refinements via client-server communication, ensuring the stability and efficiency of the R2R processes. Experimental validation on an actual manufacturing system demonstrates significant reduction in tension variation and overall improvement in production stability. The paper highlights the potential of AI-integrated digital twins as scalable solutions applicable to various industrial environments, emphasizing advancements in autonomous manufacturing technology.
