# Summary

This research paper focuses on enhancing roll-to-roll textile manufacturing processes through a combination of advanced control strategies and machine learning techniques. The primary objective is to maintain precise control over yarn speed and tension, alongside an efficient fault detection mechanism.

The approach integrates high-performance control with Gaussian Process Regression (GPR) for detecting faults efficiently, utilizing system identification, cascade control, radius adjustment compensation, and Kalman filtering. This composite solution ensures that the roll-to-roll textile systems operate smoothly under standard conditions while effectively dealing with potential issues such as yarn breaks or motor malfunctions.

The findings of experimental trials demonstrate superior performance of this integrated approach in sustaining tension regulation within tight tolerances and achieving rapid fault detection without false alarms, thus enhancing both operational precision and system resilience against critical failures.
